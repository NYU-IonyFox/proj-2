VeriMedia is an AI-powered media verification tool designed to assist journalists and fact-checkers in assessing the credibility of online content. When presented with a news article or social media post, VeriMedia cross-references the content against a curated database of verified sources and applies natural language analysis to flag potentially misleading claims. The tool generates a structured report indicating the confidence level of each claim and the supporting or contradicting evidence found. VeriMedia is intended to support, not replace, human editorial judgment, and all flagged content is reviewed by a trained human moderator before any determination is published.
